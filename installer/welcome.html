Welcome to DrumEngine01

This installer will install the DrumEngine01 plugin and its factory content on your computer.

What will be installed:

  • VST3 Plugin: /Library/Audio/Plug-Ins/VST3/DrumEngine01.vst3
  • VST Plugin: /Library/Audio/Plug-Ins/VST/DrumEngine01.vst
  • Audio Unit: /Library/Audio/Plug-Ins/Components/DrumEngine01.component
  • Presets & Samples: ~/Documents/DrumEngine01/

You can choose which plugin formats to install in the next step.

System Requirements:

  • macOS 10.13 or later
  • Compatible DAW (Digital Audio Workstation)
