Welcome to DrumEngine01

This installer installs DrumEngine01 plugins (VST3/AU/AAX) only. Factory content is installed by a separate installer.

What will be installed:

  • VST3 Plugin: /Library/Audio/Plug-Ins/VST3/
  • Audio Unit: /Library/Audio/Plug-Ins/Components/
  • AAX Plugin: /Library/Application Support/Avid/Audio/Plug-Ins/

Choose the plugin formats you want in the next step.

System Requirements:

  • macOS 10.13 or later
  • Compatible DAW (Digital Audio Workstation)
